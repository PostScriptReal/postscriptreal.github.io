Mario Kart Wii Translator

The Mario Kart Wii Translator is a simple program that converts American and PAL region names of the game's vehicles to the American versions of the names and back to the European/Australian names (Example: Sneakster -> Nitrocycle, Rally Romper -> Tiny Titan). The program was created to help avoid confusion when it came to others using the American or PAL region names of tracks and karts/bikes in Mario Kart Wii, especially when watching content revolving the game (TWD98's videos for example).
